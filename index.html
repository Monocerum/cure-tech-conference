CONFERENCE PURPOSE AND GOALS

     The New Age: Emerging Trends and Technologies in Web Development, hosted by CURE Technologies, aims to provide web developers, software engineers, and tech enthusiasts an in-depth discussion about the novel trends and technologies that shape the world of web development at present. As technology continues to evolve, several new technologies are being implemented in web development, such as but not limited to Artificial Intelligence, Progressive Web Apps, DevSecOps, and Headless CMSâ€” all of which are going to be discussed in this event.

     Experts from their respective fields will lead presentations, panel discussions, and hands-on workshops which aim to equip attendees with practical knowledge that can be applied in their web development endeavors and integrated into their web applications. The conference will ensure that no participant will leave without knowledge of the most recent technological advancements related to web development. Attendees will also have the opportunity to participate in interactive Q&A sessions after each speaker's presentation, thereby allowing participants to attain more knowledge about the specified topics.

  • The conference will provide the participants with comprehensive presentations about AI, PWAs, DevSecOps, and Headless CMS.
  • The conference will encourage participants to ask the speakers questions that the latter will answer.
  • The speakers will share their knowledge and expertise in each emerging trend.
  • The conference will feature interactive workshops hosted by each speaker to equip participants with hands-on experience of a specific emerging technology.
  • The advantages and disadvantages of AI, PWAs, DevSecOps, and Headless CMS, as well as their applications, will also be discussed by the speakers.
  • The event will provide the participants the opportunity to conduct networking with fellow attendees.

TARGET AUDIENCE

     CURE Technologies' target audience for The New Age: Emerging Trends and Technologies in Web Development are individuals who are passionate about discovering the technologies that may shape the future of web development, including Tech Students so that they may expand their knowledge of these recent trends, Web Developers who wish to stay ahead of the technological advancements and improve their skills in web development, Tech Enthusiasts who are always eager to know more about the evolution of technology, UI/UX designers who want to explore new technologies and how they can be integrated in web design, and Software Engineers that aim to enhance their comprehension of novel frameworks in web development. These are the people that will benefit that most from the event. Regardless of your level of expertise, CURE technologies will welcome you!
